I am a mathematician interested in spectral graph theory. Especially the web of analogies relating spectral graph theory, hyperbolic surfaces, random matrices, and random walks on groups. I am also interested in applications, for example, to quantum computing, tilings, or crystallography. I am especially happy when applications introduce simple examples that shed light on important theoretical phenomena. I discuss this more in my Research Statement
